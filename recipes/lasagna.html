Paneer sabzi

Description

Making lasagna can be time-consuming, but the results are well worth the wait. You'll find a ingredient list and step-by-step instructions in the recipe below, let's go over the basics: .

Ingredients

  • Meat: This super meaty lasagna has sweet Italian sausage and lean ground beef.
  • Onion and garlic: An onion and two cloves of garlic are cooked with the meat to add tons of flavor.
  • Tomato products: You'll need a can of crushed tomatoes, two cans of tomato sauce, and two cans of tomato paste.

Steps

  1. Make the meat sauce.
  2. Make the ricotta mixture.
  3. Cover with foil and bake.
  4. Let the lasagna rest before serving.
