Paneer sabzi

Description

An easy and simple, yet premium curry recipe prepared with diced paneer cubes in a spicy and tangy onion tomato gravy. Ki Sabji or Quick Paneer Curry can be an ideal gravy curry that can be served with multiple types of Indian flat breads like roti and chapati but not limited to.

It can also be a fancy side dish for flavored rice recipes like jeera rice, pulao, and fried rice.There are different types of paneer ki sabzi across India, the most popular way is dhaba-style curry. It is mainly prepared with simple onion and tomato gravy with additional thick yogurt or curd. Adding yogurt makes it thick and creamy and also helps to get that thick gravy consistency. In addition, adding cashew paste, and cooking cream makes it more fancy and more flavored.

Ingredients

  • 15 cube cottage cheese
  • 1 curry of spices
  • 2 pod cardomom

Steps

  1. firstly, in a large bowl take 15 cubes paneer, ¼ tsp turmeric, ½ tsp chilli powder, ½ tsp ginger garlic paste and ¼ tsp salt.
  2. mix well combining all the spices well.
  3. add 1 tsp cumin, 1 bay leaf, 1 pod black cardamom, 1 inch cinnamon, 2 pods cardamom and 4 cloves.
